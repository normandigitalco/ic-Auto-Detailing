Our Story

Where We Come From

IC Auto Detailing started with a straightforward mission: give Texas drivers access to honest, professional-quality detailing without the hassle of driving to a shop or waiting around. We handle everything — you just pick the time and place.

We have detailed everything from work trucks and family SUVs to BMWs, Teslas, and everything in between. No vehicle is too big, too small, or too dirty. We show up ready and we do not leave until the job is done right.

At the end of the day, this is about more than a clean car. It is about taking pride in what you drive and trusting someone to care for it the way you do.
